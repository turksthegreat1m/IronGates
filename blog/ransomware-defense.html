How Businesses Can Defend Against Ransomware

Ransomware attacks remain one of the most damaging cybersecurity threats facing organizations today. Attackers target businesses of all sizes using phishing campaigns, compromised credentials, and software vulnerabilities.

Implement Strong Backups

Organizations should maintain secure offline backups to ensure critical data can be restored without paying ransom demands.

Patch Systems Regularly

Many ransomware attacks exploit unpatched vulnerabilities in operating systems and enterprise applications.

Monitor Network Activity

Threat detection systems can help identify suspicious behavior before an attack fully executes.
