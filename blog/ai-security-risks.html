AI Security Risks Businesses Must Understand

Artificial intelligence is transforming business operations, but it also introduces new cybersecurity risks. Organizations deploying AI systems must consider how attackers may manipulate models, steal training data, or exploit automated decision systems.

Data Poisoning Attacks

Attackers may attempt to inject malicious data into AI training datasets to influence how a model behaves once deployed.

Model Theft

AI models can be valuable intellectual property and may be targeted by attackers attempting to copy or reverse engineer them.

Prompt Injection

Generative AI systems can be manipulated through crafted inputs designed to override safeguards and produce unintended responses.

Organizations should implement strong security controls around AI development, deployment, and monitoring to reduce these risks.
